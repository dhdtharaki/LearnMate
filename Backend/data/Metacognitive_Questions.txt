Here are simplified and clear questions for parents:  

1. Does your child usually stay focused on a task without getting easily distracted?  
2. Can your child plan how to complete a task on their own?  
3. Does your child remember and follow steps in the correct order when given instructions?  
4. Does your child complete chores fully without needing reminders or corrections?  
5. Can your child understand and explain what they are trying to achieve in an activity?  
6. Does your child notice and acknowledge when they make a mistake?  
7. Can your child decide which task to do first when given multiple things to do?
